About Us

We are family, integrity, community!

We are three friends who grew up in the Bay Area, who would love to see all of our communities thrive and care for one another. Bay Eats is a pop-up food truck events aimed to bring together a sense of pride and united front. Started in 2018 during the Government Shutdown, which affected many of our friends and families through out the Bay Area. During this time, the three of us started a food drive and community potlucks to help out our neighbors and bring togeter a united front. Because food is such an important part of living, we believe that everyone should never go hungry and be fed. We have community problems, which the community ourselves could solve--if we spend a little bit of our time for the benifit of each other. Since then, we have served food to more than 100,000 people. After many townhall meetings, we from Bay Eats decided to open the events to everyone from our communities, no matter what their incomes are. We asked for donation when possible. So far, we've raised more than $500,000 from donation at 5 events, which help support local charities to feed more people. Thank you for the generosity and support since the begin. We are one, we are bayers!
