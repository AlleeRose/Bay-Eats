About Us

We are family, integrity, community!

We are three friends who grew up in the Bay Area, who would love to see all of our communities thrive and care for one another. Bay Eats is a pop-up food truck events aimed to bring together a sense of pride and humanity. Bay Eats began in 2018 during the Government Shutdown, which affected many of our friends and families through out the Bay Area. During this time, the three of us started a food drive and community potlucks to feed our neighbors and bring together a united front. Because food contributes tremendously to our progress daily—whether personally, professionally, or socially—we believe that everyone should have this basic need met. No one should go hungry, when there are so many of us who care and want to share. Our communities have strength and uplifting spirit. Since then, we have served food to more than 100,000 people in need, at our weekly events hosted all throughout the Bay. After many townhall meetings and plenty of feedback, our team at Bay Eats decided to open the events to everyone from our communities, no matter what their income levels or backgrounds look like. After this implementation, we’ve seen an increase in donations and collaborations from even more of our locals. So far, we've raised more than $5,000,000 from donation, which help built more and more outreach programs aimed to transform and supplement communities in food dessert areas with healthy and nutritious alternatives. From all of us at Bay Eats and our communities, we thank you all for the generosity and support by sponsoring, donating, volunteering, cooking, or just coming to join us at our events. Bay Area and friends, you rock!
